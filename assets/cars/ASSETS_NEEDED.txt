CAR SPRITES
===========

Two sprite sheets, one per car state. Each sheet contains 8 rotation frames.

SHEET SPECS:
  Size per frame: 36x60px
  Frames per sheet: 8 (one every 45 degrees)
  Total sheet size: 288x60px (horizontal layout)
  Format: PNG with transparency

ROTATION FRAME ORDER (left to right in the sheet):
  Frame 0:  0deg   — facing NORTH (up)
  Frame 1:  45deg  — facing NE
  Frame 2:  90deg  — facing EAST (right)
  Frame 3:  135deg — facing SE
  Frame 4:  180deg — facing SOUTH (down)
  Frame 5:  225deg — facing SW
  Frame 6:  270deg — facing WEST (left)
  Frame 7:  315deg — facing NW

CAR STRUCTURE (top-down view, 36x60px frame, facing NORTH as base):
  - Main body: full 36x60 area, slightly rounded corners
  - Front windshield: ~10x4px band near top of car
  - Rear window: ~8x3px band near bottom of car
  - Trim stripe: 1px horizontal band across middle of body
  - Front wheels: 2 wheels at top-left and top-right, ~4x8px each, slightly
    wider than body edge
  - Rear wheels: 2 wheels at bottom-left and bottom-right, same size
  - Tail lights: 2 small rectangles (~3x2px) at bottom corners
