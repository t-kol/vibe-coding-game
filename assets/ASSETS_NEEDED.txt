ELMWOOD DRIFT — ASSET OVERVIEW
===============================

This folder contains all external assets for the Elmwood Drift driving game.
See subfolders for specific asset lists and generation specs.

FOLDER STRUCTURE:
  assets/
    tiles/         — Map tile sprites (64x64px each, packed into tileset.png)
    cars/          — Player car sprite sheets (rotation frames)
    buildings/     — Unique rooftop overlays for named UNO campus buildings

STYLE GUIDE (apply to everything):
  - 16-bit GTA-1 top-down pixel art
  - Tile size: 64x64px
  - UNO brand colors where appropriate: Red #C8102E, Black #1C1C1C
  - All map assets must feel cohesive — same lighting direction, same pixel density
